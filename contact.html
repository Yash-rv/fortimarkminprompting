FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Fortimark offer?

Fortimark provides a comprehensive range of digital services including web development, app development, video editing, social media management, and ad campaigns. We work with businesses of all sizes to create customized solutions that meet their specific needs and goals.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity of the work. A simple website might take 4-6 weeks, while a complex web application or e-commerce platform could take 3-6 months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide you with a detailed timeline based on your specific requirements.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer flexible pricing options including project-based pricing, retainer agreements, and hourly rates. Our pricing is transparent and based on the scope of work, timeline, and resources required. We'll provide you with a detailed proposal and breakdown of costs after our initial consultation.

Do you work with clients outside the United States?

Yes, we work with clients globally. Our team is experienced in managing remote collaborations, and we use various tools to ensure smooth communication and project management regardless of geographical locations. We've successfully completed projects for clients across North America, Europe, Asia, and Australia.

What happens after I submit my contact form?

After you submit your contact form, you'll receive an automated confirmation email. Our team will review your inquiry and get back to you within 24-48 business hours to schedule an initial consultation. During this consultation, we'll discuss your needs, answer your questions, and determine the best approach for your project.
