Cancellation Policy

Our Commitment to Flexibility

We understand that plans and priorities can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to provide clarity and reasonable flexibility for our clients while allowing us to manage our resources effectively.

Terms and Services Privacy Policy Cancellation Policy

1. Introduction

This Cancellation Policy outlines the terms and conditions for cancelling services provided by Fortimark. By engaging our services, you agree to comply with this Cancellation Policy. Please read this policy carefully to understand your rights and obligations regarding cancellations, refunds, and rescheduling of our services.

Important Note

This Cancellation Policy should be read in conjunction with our Terms and Services. In the event of any conflict between this Cancellation Policy and our Terms and Services, the Terms and Services shall prevail.

2. Project-Based Services

For one-time project-based services, such as website development, app development, or video production projects, the following cancellation terms apply:

2.1 Cancellation Before Project Commencement

If you cancel a project before we have commenced work:

  • Cancellation within 48 hours of signing the contract: Full refund of any deposit paid
  • Cancellation more than 48 hours but less than 7 days after signing the contract: Refund of deposit minus a 15% administrative fee
  • Cancellation 7 days or more after signing the contract but before work has commenced: Refund of deposit minus a 30% administrative fee

2.2 Cancellation After Project Commencement

If you cancel a project after we have commenced work:

  • You will be invoiced for all work completed up to the date of cancellation
  • Any deposit paid will be applied towards this invoice
  • If the value of work completed is less than the deposit paid, the difference will be refunded minus a 30% administrative fee
  • If the value of work completed exceeds the deposit paid, you will be required to pay the outstanding balance

2.3 Deliverables Upon Cancellation

Upon cancellation of a project and settlement of all outstanding payments, we will provide you with all completed deliverables as of the cancellation date. Incomplete work or work in progress will not be provided unless otherwise agreed upon in writing.

3. Subscription-Based Services

For ongoing subscription-based services, such as social media management, SEO services, or maintenance packages, the following cancellation terms apply:

3.1 Minimum Commitment Period

Unless otherwise specified in your service agreement, our subscription-based services have a minimum commitment period of three (3) months from the start date of the service.

3.2 Cancellation During Minimum Commitment Period

If you cancel a subscription-based service during the minimum commitment period:

  • You will be required to pay for the remainder of the minimum commitment period
  • No refund will be provided for the current billing period

3.3 Cancellation After Minimum Commitment Period

If you cancel a subscription-based service after the minimum commitment period:

  • You must provide written notice of cancellation at least 30 days before the next billing cycle
  • Services will continue until the end of the current billing period
  • No refund will be provided for the current billing period

Notice Requirement

All cancellation requests for subscription-based services must be submitted in writing to support@fortimark.co. The 30-day notice period begins on the date we receive your written cancellation request.

4. Ad Campaign Services

For digital advertising and campaign management services, the following cancellation terms apply:

4.1 Campaign Setup Fees

Campaign setup fees are non-refundable once we have commenced work on setting up your advertising campaign.

4.2 Monthly Management Fees

For ongoing campaign management services:

  • You must provide written notice of cancellation at least 14 days before the end of the current billing period
  • Services will continue until the end of the current billing period
  • No refund will be provided for the current billing period

4.3 Advertising Spend

For advertising spend managed on your behalf:

  • Any unused advertising budget that has been paid to us (and not yet paid to advertising platforms) will be refunded upon cancellation
  • Advertising spend that has already been committed to advertising platforms is subject to the refund policies of those platforms

5. Consulting and Training Services

For consulting sessions, workshops, training, and other scheduled appointments, the following cancellation terms apply:

Timing of Cancellation Refund Policy Rescheduling Option
More than 7 days before scheduled date Full refund Free rescheduling
3-7 days before scheduled date 50% refund Free rescheduling (one time only)
Less than 3 days before scheduled date No refund Rescheduling with 25% fee
No-show No refund New booking required

Rescheduling Limitations

Rescheduled sessions must be booked within 30 days of the original session date, subject to availability. Failure to reschedule within this timeframe will result in forfeiture of the service.

6. Force Majeure

Neither party shall be liable for any failure or delay in performing their obligations under this agreement due to circumstances beyond their reasonable control, including but not limited to acts of God, natural disasters, pandemic, epidemic, war, terrorism, riots, civil unrest, government action, strikes, or labor disputes.

In the event of a force majeure situation:

  • The affected party shall notify the other party as soon as reasonably possible
  • Obligations will be suspended for the duration of the force majeure event
  • Both parties will work together in good faith to find a mutually acceptable solution

7. Cancellation by Fortimark

Fortimark reserves the right to cancel or suspend services in the following circumstances:

  • Non-payment or late payment of fees
  • Violation of our Terms and Services
  • Provision of false or misleading information
  • Requests for services that are illegal, unethical, or outside our scope of expertise
  • Inability to deliver services due to unforeseen circumstances

In the event of cancellation by Fortimark:

  • We will provide you with written notice of cancellation
  • We will provide a pro-rata refund for any prepaid services that have not been delivered, unless the cancellation is due to your breach of terms or non-payment

8. Refund Process

When a refund is due according to this Cancellation Policy:

  • Refunds will be processed using the same payment method used for the original transaction, unless otherwise agreed
  • Refunds will be processed within 14 business days of the approved cancellation
  • Refund processing times may vary depending on your payment provider

Refund Requests

All refund requests should be submitted in writing to billing@fortimark.co and include your name, contact information, service details, reason for cancellation, and proof of payment.

9. Dispute Resolution

If you have a dispute regarding a cancellation or refund:

  1. Contact our customer support team at support@fortimark.co to explain your concerns
  2. If your issue is not resolved to your satisfaction, please escalate to our management team at management@fortimark.co
  3. We will make every reasonable effort to resolve the dispute amicably
  4. If we cannot reach a resolution, the dispute will be handled according to the dispute resolution provisions in our Terms and Services

10. Changes to Cancellation Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Cancellation Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated policy on our website. We will notify active clients of any significant changes to this policy via email. Your continued use of our services after any such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new Cancellation Policy.
